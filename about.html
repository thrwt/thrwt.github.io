About Me

Hello! I'm Mohamed Tharwat, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast dedicated to exploring the world of information security. I specialize in penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and security research.

Through this blog, I share my journey in cybersecurity, including writeups from various Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, lab walkthroughs, and educational content to help others learn and grow in this exciting field.

I believe in continuous learning and contributing to the security community. Whether it's solving challenges on HackTheBox, TryHackMe, or participating in bug bounty programs, I'm always seeking new ways to improve my skills.
